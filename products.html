Heifers Small Chew
Heifers Dog Chew is a traditional hard cheese also known as Churpi in the Himalayan Range. It consumed by the people of Himalayan regions of Nepal and Sikkim of India. who have known about the nutritional and dental benefits of yak/cow milk for centuries. It is produced in the mountains at more than 15000 feet, using the ancient recipes of the Himalayas with all natural products mainly lactose free milk.

Products are low fat, high protein, non GMO, Gluten free, Grain Free, No preservatives, No Artificial colours and flavours, No Binding Agents and Soy Free.

It has high nutrient values, long lasting Dog Chew which enjoy your loved pet for hours.
